About the System

Understanding the transition from static to dynamic traffic control

🚦

Smart Traffic Signal Control System

The Smart Traffic Signal Control System is a modern, AI-powered platform designed to tackle urban traffic congestion. Instead of relying on blind timers, this system continuously monitors live vehicle density across city intersections. By utilizing real-time data and artificial intelligence, it actively coordinates signal timings to ensure a continuous and smooth flow of traffic, significantly reducing wait times and vehicular emissions.

⚠️

The Current Signal Changing Problem

Traditional traffic lights operate on rigid, hardcoded timers. This creates the "Current Signal Changing Problem" — a major cause of urban bottlenecking. When a signal is fixed, it might grant 60 seconds of green light to an empty street while forcing hundreds of vehicles on an intersecting road to wait unnecessarily. This lack of adaptability leads to severe traffic jams during rush hours, increased fuel consumption, and driver frustration.

🧠

Our Intelligent Solution

We solve the static timing problem by introducing an intelligent prediction module. Based on live traffic density reports (Low, Medium, High), the system automatically allocates proportionate green light durations. If an intersection experiences high traffic, the green light duration is dynamically extended; if a lane is empty, the signal quickly shifts to accommodate other busy roads, drastically improving city-wide mobility.
